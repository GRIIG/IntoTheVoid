GRIG - ACTION REPLAY

ACTION REPLAY is a visual experience embodying the sonic realm and feelings at the origin of the eponymous EP released on TAR. In its virtual worlds, fast-paced reality encounters spatialized audio and time dilation, allowing the music to be experienced on a corporeal level.

INSTRUCTIONS :
0. Download appropriate version below
1. Unzip
2. Launch .exe (Windows) or .app (Mac)
3. Due to the Unreal Engine technical specificities, you may encounter audio/video sync issues. If that happens, just pause and resume.
4. In case of poor performances, you can try to reduce the quality in the options menu
