Inviscid
with Tendre Ael

Unconditional openness takes ourselves to a new form of immanence. By continuously involving new intelligences and subjectivities, the Whole is able to keep its momentum. Caught in perpetual motion, we are able to reconsider, reassess and evolve, altering our own perception in the process. Shifting boundaries and expanding thresholds prompt us to redefine our bonds and structures. As a continuous flow circles solid rocks, flexibility shall make us resilient.
