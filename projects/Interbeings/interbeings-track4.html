Object-Oriented Habitat
with NEW FEAR

The Whole exists across dimensions and materialities, flattening scales and temporalities. The sharing of a common purpose among each constitutive part of its ecosystem is the base of consistent evolution. The management, protection and sharing of its resources serve the long-term interest of its parts, may they be sentient or not. We shall prevent the destruction of what we value if we are to promote the creation of what we want.
